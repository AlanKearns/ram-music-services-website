Who We Are

Built for Artist Managers, Agents & Record Labels.
Delivered by Industry Insiders.

RAM Music Services was founded by a team with extensive experience working alongside internationally renowned artists and actors visiting and working in Australia. We understand the unique demands of touring Australia & New Zealand — not just logistically, but creatively, personally, and professionally.

We work with record labels, management companies and studios to execute seamless tours across Australia & New Zealand, providing trusted, on-the-ground support that removes risk and reduces complexity.

Our network spans every discipline required to deliver a tour at scale. From senior tour management, production and security through to elite local musicians, stylists, and VIP transport. Every role filled by professionals who understand live performance environments and operate to international standards.

What We Do

Complete Artist
Services

Clients can engage RAM Music Services for a single specialised service or a fully integrated end-to-end solution, depending on the needs of the project. Every service delivered by vetted industry professionals with proven live tour experience.

Artist Management

We work alongside artists as a long-term partner, helping shape direction, make smart business decisions and keep everything aligned. From deal negotiations to day-to-day coordination with agents, promoters, labels and publicists, we make sure nothing falls through the cracks.

Tour Management

We run the tour, end to end. Every date across Australia & New Zealand is planned, advanced and executed with precision. We're the central point between artist, crew, promoters and venues, keeping everything moving and everyone on the same page.

VIP Protection & Tour Security

Calm, discreet protection from people who've done this at the highest level. Our team includes ex-law enforcement and experienced touring security, fully licensed across Australia & New Zealand. We cover everything from close protection to venue security, with medical and safety training built in.

Security Driver Operations

Reliable, professional drivers who understand touring. Airport pickups, hotel runs, venue transfers, all handled smoothly and on time. A familiar, trusted presence from the moment your team lands.

Musicians & Music Directors

Access to some of the best session musicians and music directors in Australia & New Zealand. People who've played on major stages and can step straight into a tour environment.

Production

We bring together the right production setup for your show and your budget. Audio, lighting, staging, rigging, video and backline, all sourced locally to keep things efficient without cutting corners.

Press & Promotion

We connect tours with the right media across Australia & New Zealand. Radio, TV, press and digital, with scheduling and coordination handled so it fits seamlessly around the tour.

Hair & Makeup

Artists who know what works on stage and on camera. They understand long days, quick turnarounds and how to keep everything looking consistent across a full tour.

Styling & Wardrobe

Stylists who get touring. From sourcing and fittings through to show-day dressing and ongoing wardrobe management, everything is handled so the artist can just focus on performing.

Accommodation

We have strong relationships with hotels across Australia & New Zealand, from boutique stays to full-scale tour bookings. We find the right fit for your team and your budget, without the usual friction.

Hospitality

Catering and backstage setup done properly. Rider requirements, green rooms, and day to day hospitality all handled with attention to detail so everything feels taken care of. We have exclusive partnerships with Australia & New Zealand's most well-known and respected restaurants — whether it's a quick meal or a stand-up dining experience, we have you covered.

Air Travel

Touring in Australia & New Zealand is a different beast. Distances are large, routes are limited and timing matters. We plan travel with that in mind, building schedules that are realistic and efficient across cities. Through our relationships with major airlines, we secure options that work for touring parties of any size, balancing cost, comfort and flexibility.

Working Rights & Visas

We take care of visas and work permits so everything is compliant with our strict immigration laws. No stress, no last-minute issues, no guesswork.

Photography

Photographers who know how to capture a tour properly. Live shows, backstage moments and press imagery, all delivered to a professional standard.

Local Experiences

If there's something worth doing while you're here, we know about it. From low-key local spots to wildlife encounters, from scenic charters to the Bridge Climb and other unique cultural experiences — we have exclusive partnerships with some of the most iconic and unforgettable experiences that makes touring down under so special.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we contact RAM Music Services?+
The earlier the better — ideally 3–6 months before your tour dates. That said, we've successfully delivered tours with as little as 2–3 weeks notice. Don't hesitate to reach out regardless of your timeline.
Do you only work with major artists?+
Not at all. RAM Music Services works with artists at all levels of their career. We tailor our service packages to suit your budget — from emerging artists on their first Australia & New Zealand tour to global superstars on arena runs.
Can you arrange just one or two services, or is everything a package?+
You can engage RAM Music Services for a single service or the full suite — it's entirely up to you. Many clients start with just security or transport and expand to other services once they experience the quality of our team.
How do you handle artist privacy and confidentiality?+
Confidentiality is paramount at RAM Music Services. Every team member signs strict non-disclosure agreements. We never discuss, confirm, or publicise client engagements without explicit written permission.
Can you arrange working visas for international artists and crew?+
Yes. RAM Music Services manages all visa and working rights arrangements for artists and touring crew entering Australia & New Zealand, ensuring full compliance with local immigration laws.
What cities in Australia & New Zealand do you cover?+
We service all major cities and regions across Australia — Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, Gold Coast, Canberra and beyond — as well as Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch in New Zealand.
How does RAM Music Services save us money compared to bringing our own team from overseas?+
By sourcing elite, tour-experienced local professionals across every discipline, RAM Music Services eliminates the significant cost of international flights, accommodation and per diems for large overseas touring parties. Our local supplier relationships also secure preferential rates across hotels, transport and hospitality that aren't available to touring teams arriving from overseas.
Do you provide services for TV productions and events, not just tours?+
Yes. RAM Music Services has extensive experience delivering artist services within major Australian television productions and live events. Our team is equally at home in a broadcast environment as they are on a touring schedule.
